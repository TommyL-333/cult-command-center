Event Flow

How the day runs.

From early creator access to the VIP after-party, here’s what the day looks like.

10:00 AM

Invited creators get first access to the marketplace floor. Sample collection begins — VIP ticket holders get guaranteed samples from every brand before general doors open.
11:00 AM

Doors open to the public. The full marketplace, carnival activations, and stage programming are live for all ticket holders.

Live-streamed stage programming throughout the day. Creator showcases, brand spotlights, live shopping demos, and performances broadcast well beyond the crowd on the ground.

The night continues at the Felt Lounge inside MGM National Harbor. Exclusive access for VIP ticket holders. The official CCC after-party.
