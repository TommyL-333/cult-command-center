02 · The Strategy Pivot

From cold outreach to retained brand ambassadors

Stop trying to activate hundreds of one-video creators. Find 2–3 creators who are committed, on-camera talented, and willing to represent Approved Science long-term — then build the content engine around them.

01
Brand Page Publishing
Ambassadors produce the content. It posts under the Approved Science TikTok account. No personal account risk. No violation strikes. Full brand control over messaging and angle consistency.
02
Monthly Retainer — 30 Videos
Each ambassador earns $3,000/month guaranteed for a minimum of 30 videos. They know their income. You know your output. $100/video with a committed creator is cheaper and more consistent than $1,000 one-off rates.
03
Commission Still Intact
Ambassadors earn commission on every sale their content drives — even from the brand page. This gives them real skin in the game beyond the retainer. They want the videos to convert, not just post.
03 · How We Find Them

The creator challenge — earn the role

We don't just post a job listing. We run a structured, tracked challenge that attracts creators who can prove themselves before we commit. Creators must post at least 10 videos during the challenge to qualify — this filters for commitment upfront and surfaces who can actually sustain volume.

Week 1
Open Applications — Community Blast
  • Application form + role brief goes live: compensation, output expectations, brand page posting model
  • Blast across Cult Content creator community, TikTok affiliate groups, Skool communities, and wellness/supplement creator forums
  • Reacher outreach campaign to qualified supplement/wellness creators already in the system
  • Target: 50+ applicants on the kickoff call
Week 2
Kickoff Call — Lenea & Tommy Host
  • Live call for all applicants — Lenea introduces the brand and vision; Tommy walks the challenge structure
  • Challenge brief distributed: violation-safe angles, approved hooks, what the role pays, what it requires
  • Qualifier clearly stated: post a minimum of 10 videos during the 3-week window to be eligible for the role
  • Lenea's booking link shared — creators can schedule 1:1 brand calls at any point during the challenge
Weeks 2–4
Challenge Period — 10 Videos to Qualify
  • All challenge videos tracked through Reacher — volume, views, GMV, content quality scored by Cult Content
  • Fixed participation payouts for hitting the 10-video threshold — incentivizes the volume we're looking for
  • Weekly brief updates with new angles from Cult Content — iterating on what's performing
  • Creators who don't reach 10 videos exit the ambassador selection but stay in the open affiliate program
Week 5
Selection — Flexible Based on Who Performs
  • Review all qualifying creators (10+ videos) — select whoever genuinely performed well; there's no fixed quota
  • If one creator is outstanding → hire one. If three are strong → hire three. Quality drives the number, not a pre-set budget
  • Offer ambassador agreements: $3,000/mo for 30 videos/month, posting through Approved Science brand pages
  • Month 2 begins: ambassadors onboarded, content calendar built, brand page posting starts

06 · The Bigger Picture

From ambassador program to affiliate flywheel

The ambassador program isn't just a content play — it's the R&D lab for the entire organic affiliate strategy. Here's how it compounds.

Step 1
Prove safe angles
Ambassadors test formats on the brand page. Brand-page posts don't threaten creator accounts. High-output volume lets us iterate fast.
Step 2
Identify what converts
30–90 videos/month generates real data — which hooks get views, which angles drive add-to-cart, which formats get flagged.
Step 3
Distribute proven briefs
Winning, violation-safe formats become the brief we send to open affiliates. They're not improvising — they're replicating what's already proven to work and be safe.
Step 4
Organic volume scales
Affiliates who know what to post, know it's safe, and can see it converting are far more likely to post consistently. The violation problem largely solves itself.
🚀
The compounding effect: Today we have 72 active creators generating $532/month because they don't know what to post and don't want to risk their accounts. In 90 days, with proven violation-safe angles, those same 72 creators have a brief they can confidently follow. The ambassador program doesn't replace the affiliate channel — it unlocks it.
