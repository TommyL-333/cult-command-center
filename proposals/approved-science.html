What's blocking creator volume
  • Violation risk deters creators — posting about "Appetite Suppressant" triggers TikTok's health content filters; creators protect their own accounts first
  • Scale ceiling on cold outreach — 2,000 outreach messages yielding ~5 active creators is an unsustainable ratio
  • No face for the brand — Marketily team doesn't have on-camera creators internally, and paying per-video ($1,000+) isn't scalable
  • AI-generated video fell short — Arcads demos didn't translate to usable outputs; the product shots and body movements weren't realistic enough
What IS working

💡
The insight: The violation problem is not an Approved Science problem — it's a structural limitation of creator-owned accounts. The fix is simple: move content onto your brand pages. When creators post through Approved Science's TikTok account, the violation risk shifts to the brand-level compliance review, not their personal creator score. Creators can post freely. You control the volume.
02 · The Strategy Pivot

From scattered affiliates to retained brand ambassadors

Instead of trying to activate hundreds of cold creators who post once and disappear, we identify 2–3 creators who are talented, committed, and willing to represent Approved Science full-time — then pay them like employees.

01
Brand Page Publishing
Ambassadors create the content, but it posts under the Approved Science TikTok account. No personal account risk. No violation strikes. Full creative control over brand messaging.
02
Monthly Retainer Model
Each ambassador earns a guaranteed monthly retainer ($3,000/mo) with a minimum video output requirement — typically 25 videos/month. They know exactly what they're making. You know exactly what you're getting.
03
Commission Layer Still Intact
Ambassadors can still earn commission on sales from their content. This gives them real skin in the game on top of the base retainer — they want the videos to convert, not just go up.
✅
Why this works for Approved Science: You don't need 200 creators posting once each. You need 2–3 creators posting 25 times each, every month, compounding over time. A creator on a $3,000/mo retainer delivering 25 videos is paying $120/video for talent that's bought into your brand and improving with every post. That's cheaper than Jenna's rate — and far more sustainable.
03 · How We Find Them

The creator challenge — a competition to find your ambassadors

We don't just post a job listing. We run a structured challenge that attracts creators who are already motivated, already on TikTok, and willing to prove themselves for a long-term role. The challenge is how we find the top 2–3 out of a broad field.

Week 1
Open Applications — Community Blast
  • Application form + brief goes live: what the role is, what they'll earn, what's expected
  • Blast across Cult Content creator community, TikTok affiliate groups, Skool communities, and online creator forums
  • Reacher outreach campaign to qualified creators in the supplement/wellness space
  • Target: 50+ applicants — enough to select the best 2–3 for the challenge period
Week 2
Kickoff Call — Live Onboarding
  • Lenea and Tommy host a live call for all applicants — introduces the brand, the product, the program
  • Challenge brief distributed: approved scripts, content angles, brand guidelines, violation avoidance tips
  • Each participant commits to posting at least 1–2 videos during the 2-week challenge window
  • Fixed participation payouts for completing challenge — incentivizes execution over just applying
Weeks 2–3
Challenge Period — Tracked Performance
  • All challenge videos tracked through Reacher — views, GMV, engagement, content quality
  • Lenea can connect directly with standout creators for relationship building (calendar link distributed)
  • Weekly check-in message sequence to keep participants active and posting
  • Tommy and team review every video for content quality, brand fit, and violation risk
Week 4
Selection + Onboarding as Ambassadors
  • Review all challenge results — select top 2–3 creators based on content quality, posting consistency, and audience fit
  • Offer long-term ambassador agreements — $3,000/mo retainer, 25 videos/month minimum, brand page publishing
  • Rejected participants still remain in the affiliate program — they can keep posting on commission
  • Month 2 begins: ambassadors onboarded, content calendar built, first brand-page videos scheduled

07 · Operational Upgrades

Changes starting immediately

Separate from the ambassador program — these are fixes to how we work together that take effect now.

Biweekly 1:1 Calls

Group calls are being replaced with biweekly 1:1s between Tommy and Lenea. This allows us to actually go deep on blockers specific to Approved Science — not split 30 minutes across 5–10 brands. The feedback loop is faster and the decisions we make are sharper.

Creator Contact Sharing

Lenea will receive direct contact info for all active creators in the Approved Science pipeline — names, handles, Discord/WhatsApp contacts. Lenea's relationship-building (Jenna, Candy, etc.) is a real competitive advantage. We should be enabling it, not bottlenecking it through our team.

Lenea's Booking Calendar

Lenea creates a Calendly (or similar) booking link that gets distributed to all creators. Any creator who wants a 1:1 call to discuss the brand, their content, or their strategy can book time directly with Lenea. This converts interested creators into engaged ones.

Single Product Focus

For the first 90 days, all ambassador content, briefs, and ad spend go exclusively toward Appetite Suppressant. It has the strongest GMV signal and the highest commercial intent. Once we have repeatable content formats proven out, we'll expand the playbook to Collagen and Paris Strand.
