02 · Unit Economics

The math behind every sale

$54 list price, ~$8 COGS. Factoring in a 26% promo to land at $40 (matching Amazon parity) and a 6% GMV Max budget layered on top of organic affiliate costs.

How to improve margins further
  • AOV bundles: A 2-unit pack at ~$130 list roughly doubles net contribution per transaction — the single highest-leverage lever
  • Organic-first: Every sale driven by an organic affiliate post costs $0 in ad spend — ads should amplify proven content, not substitute for it
  • Promo discipline: Reserve flash sales for strategic windows (platform events, new creator waves) rather than running constant discounts
  • Creator quality: One creator driving $10k/month at 15% open commission generates more profit than 50 creators who post once and stop
