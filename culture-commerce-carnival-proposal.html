The Event

What is the Culture
Commerce Carnival?

CCC is an outdoor marketplace event at National Harbor — 3,000+ people, one day, one waterfront. The core is 1,000 application-only social commerce creators matched to 100 curated product vendors. Around that is a full public carnival: live music, entertainment, food, and brand activations drawing walk-up traffic from one of the DMV’s busiest destinations. The whole thing is live-streamed.

Creators get in free. Vendors are curated. Every vendor brings 200 product samples. The result is a room where commerce actually happens — not just a networking event with badge lanyards.
